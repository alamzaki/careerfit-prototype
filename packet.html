Executive Summary

🧭 The student problem: Students often freeze when it’s time to choose a career. They collect courses, certificates, and advice but feel lost when judging what’s truly worth pursuing. They crave a clear, trusted signal about “where they fit” and what to do next.

🏫 The university problem: Career service teams are under pressure to demonstrate outcomes. It’s no longer enough to say “we taught skills.” Schools must show clear links between their programs and real jobs students land.

💡 Our idea: A lightweight tool that instantly suggests 3–5 market-aligned career options and nudges the student into the next action (micro-skills, projects, or applications). Quick, credible, and motivating.

🔬 How we’re testing: Start with one hook — the Career Fit Quiz. If students finish it and request their full report, we know they find value. That’s our demand signal.

Concept Map

🎯 Target Users

Primary: Students (18–26) who are ambitious but stuck in “career paralysis.” They’ve tried learning and advice but still can’t choose a confident path.

Secondary: University career services that want data-driven guidance tools to improve placement satisfaction and employer trust.

🎁 Value Proposition

  • ✨ For students: Fast clarity — 3–5 options linked to their strengths and actual job demand, plus one small action to take immediately.
  • 📊 For universities: A shift from “teaching skills” to “guiding careers,” with outcome data that strengthens credibility with employers and students.
Students: clear and confident about next steps.
Universities: measurable reporting on student outcomes.

🧭 Five Big Features

  1. Career Fit Quiz: 6 quick questions → instant 3–5 matches.
  2. Market Demand Dashboard: Shows salaries, growth, and demand for each path.
  3. Recruiting Tracker: A central place for job applications and interviews.
  4. Peer Benchmarking: Anonymous “people like you also explored” insights.
  5. Action Roadmap: Bite-sized skills and projects tailored to each career path.

Experiment Build

Why this build: It’s the leanest way to test demand fast. Instead of coding complex dashboards, we made a quiz + simple report. If students care, they’ll opt in. If not, we pivot quickly.

🧪 What’s in the Prototype

  • Landing Page: clear promise (“Find your career fit in under 2 minutes”) with one big CTA.
  • Career Fit Quiz: 6 short questions (values, strengths, goals).
  • Instant Suggestions: 3 career options shown right away; email unlock for full details.
  • Quick Feedback: thumbs-up/down on whether results “felt right.”

📏 Metrics & Decisions

  • Primary: Quiz → Email conversion = demand.
  • Secondary: Thumbs-up rate = trust. School leads = viability.

🧭 Decision Rules

  • Positive: ≥25% conversion → build MVP with dashboard + tracker, pilot with 1–2 universities.
  • Negative: <12% conversion or low trust → try different hook (e.g., tracker-first), refine quiz, re-test.
