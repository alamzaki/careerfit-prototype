This page is a polished public experiment write-up that demonstrates the concept, rationale, thresholds, and decision framework behind testing a career-fit quiz as a student engagement tool.

Executive Summary

Why it matters: Career choice anxiety is a silent burden for students, while universities struggle to validate outcomes. This micro-intervention aims to provide clarity in two minutes, reduce wasted years, and strengthen institutional trust with parents, employers, and ranking agencies.

This is more than a quiz. It is a test of whether a simple interactive surface can spark measurable engagement and serve as a foundation for scalable, evidence-based career services.

Students: overwhelmed by too many career choices and generic advice.
Universities: pressured to prove actual career outcomes beyond skills taught.
