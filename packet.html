Executive Summary

🧭 The student problem (in plain words): Choosing a career is confusing. There’s a lot of generic advice, many paths, and it’s hard to judge the real return on time and money. Students want quick clarity and a next step they can trust.

🏫 The university problem: Career teams must show outcomes, not just “skills taught.” They need simple signals about where students fit and how to guide them toward roles with demand.

💡 My idea: A lightweight tool that gives students 3–5 market-aware matches in minutes, then nudges them into small, concrete actions (micro-skills or starter projects).

🔬 How I’m testing it: I lead with one hook — A short quiz. If students complete it and request a full report, that’s proof they care and want more.

Concept Map

🎯 Target Users

Primary: students (18–26) who are ambitious but overwhelmed — they’ve done courses and heard advice, but still can’t pick a path with confidence.

Secondary: university career services that want to guide students toward real roles and improve placement satisfaction.

🎁 Value Proposition

  • For students: quick clarity — 3–5 best-fit options matched to strengths and market demand, plus one small next step to try today ✨.
  • For universities: a shift from “we teach skills” to “we guide careers,” with simple outcomes they can show and measure 📊.
Students — clarity and action in minutes.

🧭 Five Big Features

  1. Career Fit Quiz (Hook): 6 questions → instant 3–5 options.
  2. Market Demand Dashboard: salary bands, demand level, and growth outlook.
  3. Recruiting Tracker: one place for applications, interviews, and referrals.
  4. Peer Benchmarking: anonymous “people like you also explored…” insights.
  5. Action Roadmap: micro-skills and starter projects for each path.

Experiment Build

Why this build: It’s the smallest possible surface to read demand quickly. If students find value, they ask for the full report; if not, we learn fast and adjust.

🧪 What’s in the Prototype

  • Landing Page: one clear promise (“Find your career fit in under 2 minutes”) and a single CTA.
  • Career Fit Quiz: 6 short questions covering values, strengths, and work style.
  • Instant Suggestions: 3 paths shown immediately; email unlock for the full report (salary, demand, next steps).
  • Quick Feedback: thumbs-up/down on the “feel” of results.

📏 Metrics & Decisions

  • Primary: Quiz → Email conversion (demand).
  • Secondary: thumbs-up rate (accuracy feel) and “For Schools” interest (viability).

🧭 Decision Rules

  • Positive: ≥ 25% conversion → build MVP-1 (market dashboard + recruiting tracker) and run a small pilot with 1–2 departments.
  • Negative: < 12% or low thumbs-up → try a different hook (e.g., lead with Recruiting Tracker), refine copy/quiz, and re-test.
